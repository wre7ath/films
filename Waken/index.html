Synopsis

Khatereh has made a major decision. She asks for help from someone who might help her for commiting to this decision.

The production of the short film Waken took about a year and a half from the initial idea to the last stage of production (November 2021). The filmmakers of this work have personally prepared the entire budget of the film. One of the challenges of making this film is that the locations of the film take place outside. With the special conditions of the corona epidemic. Waken is the second official production of its filmmakers after the short film "The Hand". The film is produced by Falch Arts, an independent platform made by filmmakers, Amir Kalateh, Nasser Amin.
