Synopsis

The film is about the story of a girl, Khatereh, who has decided to commit suicide. After finding a deadly pill, she goes to an address she got from someone, where she meets the seller. The main part of the story takes place at night. Night atmosphere, silence and proper lighting of midnight space. The main theme of the film is the overnight metamorphosis of Khatereh and of course the drama between her and the drug dealer that causes this metamorphosis.

The two main characters in the film are “Khatereh” and “Ali Poker”, drama takes place between them. Khatereh is a quiet and determined girl who wants to end her life, on the other hand, Ali Poker (drug dealer), is a talkative and perhaps a little mysterious. Ali Poker recalls that they have to go to another address to get the pill, and because the destination is remote, they drive to there. Ali Poker has a mysterious and alternating personality. It may not be possible to tell if he is a good (white) or a bad person, and maybe a bit of both. Even at the end of the film, it cannot be said that Ali Poker is essentially a swindler or a savior. The main decision maker is Khatereh and those who watch the film. But apart from all this, the final event is more important than the character of Ali Poker. Something that changes Khatereh forever.
